Position Summary

Nmu Nmu serves as the Director of Digital Innovation & Student Organizations at National Management & Technology University (NMU). In this dual role, Nmu is responsible for overseeing the integration of modern collaborative technologies into the student extracurricular ecosystem.

Current focus includes the digital transformation of student society management and the implementation of enterprise-grade productivity tools for student-led initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Oversight of official Student Organizations (including the nmu society).
  • Management of University-wide digital tool licenses and academic discounts.
  • Coordinating with the IT department for workspace infrastructure.
  • Lead Administrator for the nmu organization's external collaborations (Notion, GitHub, Slack).

Department & Affiliation
